About Us

Our Mission

We offer a suitable database which has a variety of tree species from around the World. We increase access to the public who want to plant more trees in their environment. Our mission is to increase the area of tree cover in the country to reduce the rate of irregular rainfall patterns and also to fight global warming.

Our Vision

Working together hand in hand to make our country and the world a greener place

Fun Fact

With only 7% of the country having tree cover, Kenya is the least forested country in Africa. That works out as 67 trees per person rather than a global average of 420.
